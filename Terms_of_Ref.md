# Terms of Reference: Phael Flor Digital Catalog App

## 1. Project Overview
The goal is to develop a high-performance, lightweight mobile application for **Phael Flor Madagascar**. The app serves as a digital product showcase for their three main brands (Phael Flor, Actiflora, Florentine). 

## 2. Objectives
* Provide a professional mobile interface to browse the product catalog.
* Organize products by Brand and Usage Tags (Cooking, Health, Hygiene, Well-being).
* Minimize app size using cloud-based data and image hosting.
* Offer detailed technical and usage information for B2B and B2C users.

## 3. Targeted Users
* **B2B Partners:** International buyers looking for technical characteristics of oils/spices.
* **Consumers:** Local and international users looking for usage tips (recipes, wellness).

## 4. Technical Constraints
* **Framework:** Flutter (Cross-platform iOS/Android).
* **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL + Cloud Storage).
* **Mode:** Read-only (No e-commerce/payment gateway required).
* **Language:** French - English - Malagasy
